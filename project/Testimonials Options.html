Three ways to make it move.

Same six client quotes, three interaction models. Try each, then tell me which to drop into the homepage — or mix elements.

option 01

Auto-scroll marquee

An endless ribbon that drifts on its own and pauses when you hover a card. Scales to any number of quotes. On-brand for the system's testimonial strip.

option 02

Featured + client selector

One big quote in focus; a roster of clients beside it. Click a name to swap the feature, or let it auto-advance. Editorial and deliberate.

option 03

Draggable carousel

Two cards in view, swipe or drag to page through, with arrows and an active indicator. Tactile and familiar.
